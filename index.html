About Me

I am a current student at Columbia University in the City of New York studying computer science, with a particular interest in computer graphics, artificial intelligence, and the fusion of art and computation. My interests outside of the field include literature, philosophy, music, art, history, and psychology. I also engage in long-distance running, cycling, and the occasional game of tennis.

This web site hosts a collection of projects I am currently working on, drawings, selected writings, and a mishmash of various math and coding exercises I'm working on. If you have any inquiries about my work, please do not hestiate to contact me using the Contact link at the top or bottom of this page.
